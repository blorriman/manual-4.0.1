Once you have voting, you can change your vote at any time prior to the end of the Meeting _(this is only available with **Blind** voting)_.

:::success Info
LORROS defaults to **Blind** voting; however, the Group Admin can select **Visible** and/or **Secret** voting.
Learn more about how LORROS handles the different ballot options _([click here](../during/voting#ballot-options))_.

:::

### Participants

In **Blind** voting _(default)_, the Members who have voted are shown as **Participants**.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/meetings/participants.gif')} />
</center>

:::success Info
If **Visiible** voting is selected, the results of the voting is displayed.
:::
