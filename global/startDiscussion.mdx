> **Discussion:** _The action or process of talking about something in order to reach a decision or to exchange ideas._

:::success Info
A **Discussion** should be about "_exchnaging ideas_". If you just want to send a quick email, use the [Group Email](/docs/started/groupHome#send-an-email) on the **Group Home Page**.
:::

> 1. Enter the **Subject** of the **Discussion**.
> 1. Enter the **Background**
> 1. Pick an **End Date** _(no one like an endless Discission)_.
> 1. **Extras**:
>    1. Add any [**Files**](../../docs/started/generalUsage#add-files-to-submissions) if required.
>    1. You can also add a [**Poll**](../../docs/started/generalUsage#add-polls).
>    1. Group **Admin** can invite [**Guests**](../../administration/discussions/extend#add-guests) to participate.
