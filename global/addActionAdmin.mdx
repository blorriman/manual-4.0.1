**Actions** can be added to Discussions or from the Group Home page. The Group **Admin** can assign the Action to any of the Group Members; however, Actions created by non-admin Members are automatically assigned to them _(they cannot assign Actions to other Members)_.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/global/addActionAdmin.gif')} />
</center>

:::caution Notice
**Actions** will remain visible to the Group until they are marked as **Completed** by either the Member it was assigned to, or the Group Admin.
:::
