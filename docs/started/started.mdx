# First Steps

### Self Register

If you are not registered with **LORROS**, click the register link at the bottom of the Log In form. We require your email address, full name and password _(must be at least 6 characters)_.

:::success Personal Pronouns
You can add the [**Personal Pronouns**](https://www.mypronouns.org) that you want to be referred to by in the singular third person _(ie: She/Her)_ by adding them after your name. They will appear with your name throughout **LORROS**.
:::

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/started/registration-2.gif')} />
</center>

Once you have filled the form and registered you will be required to confirm your email. An email will be sent to you with a confirmation link – clicking the link finishes the registration process and opens your home page.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/started/register email.png')} />
</center>

### Invitation to **LORROS**

When an invitation is sent out from a group, you will receive an email with a button to **Accept** or **Decline** the invitation, click the button and follow the instructions.
