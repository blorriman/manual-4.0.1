A **LORROS** user profile is comprised of your **EMAIL** address _(all **LORROS Messages and Notices** will be sent to this address)_, **NAME** _(this is not social media so it is recommended that real names be used)_, short **BIO**, **PASSWORD** _(minimum 6 characters)_, **AVATAR**, and preferred **LANGUAGE** _(English or French)_.

:::success Info
All of this information can be updated from **Your Homepage** at any time.
:::

### Change Name

It is always best pactice to use your real name, but you can change your name in **LORROS** at any time.

---

### Change Email

When you receive an invite to participate in a **LORROS Group**, you must create your own account. At that time, you can choose your preferred email address for all future **LORROS** communications.
You can change your **LORROS** email address at any time _(you are the only one with access to your profile)_.

---

### Change Password

**LORROS** easily allows you to change your password at any time.

---

### Change Avatar

It's always fun to add a small photo _(or avatar)_ of yourself which will be displayed beside all your comments.

:::caution Caution
It is best to use a square image _(not greater than 250px X 250px)_.
:::

---

### Language

**LORROS** is currently availabe in English and French. Your language will be automatically detected when you register, but you can change it any time.

---

### Unregister from Lorros

:::warning Warning
Unregistering from **LORROS** will delete your profile and remove you from all groups permanently!
:::

You may delete your **LORROS** profile at any time. From your home page, click _“Unregister”_ button. This will remove you from the **LORROS** database and all **LORROS Groups** to which you belong.

:::warning Caution
If you are the sole Administrator in any **LORROS Group**, they will be deleted unless you assign another Administrator.
:::

If you are the last member of a **LORROS Group**, the Group will be removed from **LORROS** and all the group’s records will be deleted from the **LORROS** archives.

## Current Activities

If you have any current Activites _(**Discussions**, **Meetings**, **Actions**)_ in any of your Groups, they appear on your Home page. The name of the Group, followed by the Subject of the Activity and the end date. If there are more than 2 Activities _(ie: Discussions)_ click the + button to see all Activities.

## Start a New Group

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/started/startNewGroup.png')} />
</center>

**LORROS** allows you to start as many new Groups as you need. When You start a new Group, you are automatically registered as the **Admin** of the Group. To learn how to start a new Group [click here](../../administration/groups/groups).

:::warning Notice
A new Group is independent of all other Groups. If you want to have Groups connected _(affilated)_, learn more about **Affilated Groups** [here](../../administration/groups/affiliates).
:::
