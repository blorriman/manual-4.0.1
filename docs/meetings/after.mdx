# Post Meeting

## Meeting Ending Notice

Aproximately 24 hours before the Meeting will end, you will recieve an email notofying you that the Meeting will be ending. This gives you an opportunity to add any final Comments and make sure you have voted.

## Printing

Even though **LORROS** Meetings are always available online, they can also be printed as a pdf document.

### Minutes

The Meetings **Minutes** are just the Agenda Items and the results of any Motions. These Minutes can be distributed and/or posted as required by your bylaws.

### Transcript

The **Transcript** includes all the Comments and Replies made by the Group Members. Transcripts may be submitted under some 'Open Meeting' regulations - <span className='error'>but check your local regulations first.</span>

## Archives

All **LORROS** Meetings are available in the [Group Archives](../started/groupHome#archives) when the Meeting is over.
