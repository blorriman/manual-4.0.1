# Guests

> **Guests** _(not belonging to the Group)_ can be invited by the Group Admin to participate in **Discussions** and individual **Agenda Items**. Guests are granted special access to the Comments/Replies and files while the activity is active.

## The Invite

When you are invited to participate, you will receive the folowwing invite which you can **Accept** or **Decline** by clicking the button.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/guests/guestInvite.jpg')} />
</center>

### Accept

You will be given the opportunity to **Accept** or **Decline** the Invitation. If you Accept the invitation, you will be asked for your Name and to create a Password _(unless you are already registered in **LORROS**)_.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/guests/guestInvite.gif')} />
</center>

## Guest Home Page

If you have only one invite the Home Page will open that Activity. If you have more than one invite, simply click the Activity. To return back to the **Guest Home** page, click the **My Guest Invites** tab.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/guests/guestHome.gif')} />
</center>

## Meetings

If you have been invited to a Group Meeting, you will opnly have access to the Agenda Item that you were invited to. If you no longer wish to participate in the Meeting, click the Delete icon _(trash can)_ in the upper right. This will remove you as a Guest in the Meeting.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/guests/guestMeeting.jpg')} />
</center>

## Discussions

If you no longer wish to participate in the Discussion, click the Delete icon _(trash can)_ in the upper right. This will remove you as a Guest in the Discussion.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/guests/guestDiscussion.jpg')} />
</center>

## Observer Only

You may be invited to an Activity as an **Observer** _(no comments)_. If so, you will see an icon beside the Subject line at the top.

If the Comments of the Activity have been **Disabled** by the Group Admin _(includes all Group Members)_, you will see both the icon beside the Subject line at the top as well as an Alert Message at the bottom.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/guests/enableDisable.gif')} />
</center>

## Comments & Replies

### Adding Comments

**Comments** should be relative to the **Activity** _(**Replies** should be specific to a **Comment**)_.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/global/addingComments-2.gif')} />
</center>

### Add Files

Simply click the **Add File** tab and follow the instructions.

<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/global/addFiles-2.gif')} />

The **Files** are accessible from both the Files tab at the top of the Activity as well as the Paper Clip icon next to the Comment.

### Adding Replies

When Replying to a Comment, you will see the Reply To This Comment tab. When you have added your Reply, you can also [add Files](#add-files) before submitting your Reply.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/global/addingReplies-2.gif')} />
</center>

Simply click the **Add File** tab and follow the instructions.

The **Files** are accessible from both the Files tab at the top of the Activity as well as the Paper Clip icon next to the Comment.

## End of Session

When the Activity _(**Discussion** or **Meeting**)_ is coming to an end, you will get an email notifying you that the Activity will be ending so that you will get a chance to add final Comments.

Once the Activity has ended, you will also be notified and you will no longer have access.
