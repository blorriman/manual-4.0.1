# Drafts

As soon as you start any Activity _(Discussion, Meeting or Action)_, it is saved as a **Draft** until you Submit it. The Draft icon will show up in the Sidebar at the top and in the Group tab.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/drafts/drafts-1.gif')} />
</center>

### Opening Drafts

When you go to the Home page of the Group that you created the Draft in, you will see the Draft tab at the top - click the tab.

To open the Draft, simple click Draft that you want to work on.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/drafts/drafts-2.gif')} />
</center>

### Deleting Drafts

To **Delete** a Draft, click the Delete icon _(trash can)_.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/drafts/drafts-3.gif')} />
</center>
