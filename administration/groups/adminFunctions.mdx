### Add Resources

**Group Resources** are files that are specific to the Group _(rather than an Activity)_ such as Bylaws, Rules & Regulations, etc.

Uploading the files is the same as [Adding Files to Submissions](../../docs/started/generalUsage#add-files-to-submissions)
