# Proxy Votes

**Proxy Votes** are available to the Group **Admin**. The Group Admin can Move, Second and Vote on any Motion as a Proxy from another Voting Member. Proxy Votes are marked with an asterisks '**\***' and on hover will display who Voted the Proxy Vote.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/administration/meetings/proxy-1.gif')} />
</center>

:::warning Caution
A **Proxy Vote** cannot be changed by the Admin; however, the Member can change their Vote whether it was Proxied or not _(if the Ballot type is Blind)_.
:::

---

## Not Present

The Admin can also record a Member as **Not Present**; however, if a Member has Moved or Seconded _(even if by Proxy)_ they cannot be recorded as Not Present.

:::warning Attention
A Member recorded as Not Present may affect the ability of the Motion to reach Quorum and therefore should be considered carefully by the Admin.
:::
