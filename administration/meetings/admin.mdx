# Admin Tab

There are addition functions the **Admin** can do during the Meeting that are accessed from the **Admin Tab**.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/administration/meetings/admin-tabs-1.gif')} />
</center>

---

## Voting Options

The **Voting Options** section allows you to change the **Ballot Types** [see **Ballots**](./ballots). The Ballot Type is displayed in the Voting Tab at the top of the Meeting.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/administration/meetings/admin-tabs-2.gif')} />
</center>

:::warning Caution
Once you select **Visible** or **Secret**, it cannot be changed back.
:::

---

## Edit Date

The Dates of the Meeting can be changed at any time. You may want to end the Meeting earlier tha planned, or extend the Meeting to give Members more time.

To activate, click the Date you wish to change.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/administration/meetings/admin-tabs-3.gif')} />
</center>

---

### Start Date

Changing the **Start Date** is pretty straight forward, to start the Meeting immediately, simple set the Start Date to the current day/time or before. Once the Meeting has started you will no longer have access to the Start Date.

---

### End Date

Changing the **End Date** is also straight forward except the End Date must be at least an hour in the future.

---

## Comments Enabled/Disabled/Anonymous

There may be times when you want to **Disable** Comments _(during a live or video Meeting for instance)_, or you may want the Comments to be **Anonymous**.

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/administration/meetings/mtg-anony-1.gif')} />
</center>

:::warning Caution
If you **Disable** Comments, be sure to remember to **Enable** them again.
:::
