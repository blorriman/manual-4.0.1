# Draft Agenda

Prior to the Meeting starting, the **Draft Agenda** is available for Members to review and add additional Agenda Items. The **Admin** can also edit or delete Agenda Items, as well as delete the entire Meeting prior to the Meeting starting.

:::note Note
If there are any **Referrals** or **Deferred** Agenda Items, they will appear _(to the Admin only)_ in the Draft Agenda.
:::

<center>
	<img src={useBaseUrl('/img/administration/meetings/draft-1.jpg')} />
</center>

## Adding Agenda Items

If the **Meeting** hasn't started, any Member can add Agenda Items to the Draft Agenda.

:::success Info
Agenda items can only be added before the Meeting starts. Once the Meeting starts the Agenda is fixed.
:::

:::success Info
Agenda items can be edited and/or deleted by the Group Admin or the Member who created the Agenda item before the Meeting starts.
:::
